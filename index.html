Texas Deep Net is intended to educate students about machine learning and blockchain to provide them with hands-on experience building machine learning models. We strive to push students to apply their knowledge and gain experience by building applications that solve real-world problems.

Machine Learning

Fall semester is focused on teaching our members machine learning concepts while students complete an introductory course. During Spring, members will be placed in groups in order to complete their projects.

Blockchain

TDN will be hosting one blockchain conference towards the end of each semester. In addition, TDN will partner with other organizations to build decentralized applications that can improve the operations of those organizations.

Self Development

During each meeting, a portion of time will be reserved to learning about different topics of self-development including mindset, productivity, and financial literacy. TDN strives for growth in both technical and soft skills.
